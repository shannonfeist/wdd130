Overview

Purpose

We are creating this website to build revenue. This site will help us to reach more people and to share the products we have to offer. This site will also allow customers to place orders and to scan a QR code for payments. We will share how we started and who we are.

Audience

Our audience is individuals of all ages who enjoy good quality handmade products. We will target pet owners with our handmade collars and leashes. Most of our audience will access our site on a mobile device or laptop.
